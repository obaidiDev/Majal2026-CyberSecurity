Everyone can lock (c = mᵉ mod n), but the public key can't undo itself — only d reverses it, and no secret ever crossed the wire. Here d is easy to find because n = 5 × 11. Real keys use an n hundreds of digits long: no computer can factor it, so no one can recover d.
n = 5 × 11 → p=5, q=11
φ = (5−1)(11−1) = 40
d = e⁻¹ mod φ = 3⁻¹ mod 40 = 27  (since 3 × 27 = 81 = 2×40 + 1)
The whole secret rests on factoring n. Trivial at 55 — impossible at 600 digits.
